## Form
- **Novel.** Not memoir, not novella, not script. Target ~80–100k words.
- Fully fictionalized: characters, places, situations, feelings, decisions.
- **Fake names.** Real identities protected in every output.

## Scope
- The whole 5-year relationship, plus the 11 years in the UK.
- **UK is a co-subject.** Confirmed 2026-04-19. The BRP refusal, the court case, the toxic London job, and the Kennedy firing establish the UK as a parallel antagonist — a place the narrator loves that rejects him, bureaucratically and personally. Symmetry with Lera is deliberate.
- **Istanbul is the final act**, not the whole book.

## Structure
- **Teleological timeline.** Linear forward. Every chapter bends toward the Istanbul ending.
- No dual timeline. No braided structure. No Istanbul frame.
- **Opens:** May Dip 2019, St Andrews — the day they met.
- **Closes:** Istanbul, April 2025.
- Flashbacks permitted, not centered.

## POV
- **First person.** Still open until the opening scene is drafted both ways and compared.
- Retrospective voice — narrator in the present writing about the past. Catcher-style *framing* only, not Catcher-style *voice*.

## Engine
- **Restoration of the pre-Sergeyeva self.**
- The narrator's unadmitted want drives the plot — what he can't face, not what he claims he wants.
- Self-pity is the failure mode. Watch for it every scene.

## Theme — LOCKED 2026-04-19
